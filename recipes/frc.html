Fried Rice

Description:

Veg Fried Rice is a popular Chinese-inspired dish featuring cooked rice stir-fried with a variety of vegetables, soy sauce, and other seasonings.

Common vegetables used include carrots, peas, corn, cabbage, and onions. Some recipes also include eggs for added protein.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked rice
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Carrot, diced
  • Peas
  • Cabbage, finely chopped
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • Garlic paste
  • Ginger paste
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Sesame oil

Steps:

  1. Prepare vegetables: Heat oil in a wok or pan. Add chopped onions, carrots, and cabbage. Stir-fry until slightly tender.
  2. Add flavor: Add garlic and ginger paste. Stir-fry for a few seconds.
  3. Combine: Add cooked rice and mix well.
  4. Season: Pour soy sauce and salt according to taste.
  5. Finish: Garnish with green onions.
