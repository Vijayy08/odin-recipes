Paneer Butter Masala

Description:

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich, creamy Indian curry featuring soft paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a luscious tomato-based gravy.

This dish is often served with naan or roti, and it's a popular choice among vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike. The combination of flavors and textures makes it a truly satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of paneer, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of garam masala powder
  • 1/2 cup of tomato puree
  • 1/4 cup of ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon chilli powder
