Paneer Butter Masala

Description:

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich, creamy Indian curry featuring soft paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a luscious tomato-based gravy.

This dish is often served with naan or roti, and it's a popular choice among vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike. The combination of flavors and textures makes it a truly satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer, cubed
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Tomato, pureed
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chilies, chopped
  • Cashew paste
  • Butter
  • Ghee
  • Garam masala
  • Coriander powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Cumin seeds
  • Kasoori methi
  • Coriander leaves, chopped
  • Cream
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Sauté the base: Heat ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions, ginger-garlic paste, and green chilies. Sauté until golden brown.
  2. Blend the gravy: Add tomato puree, cashew paste, and spices (coriander, turmeric, red chili). Cook until oil separates.
  3. Cook the paneer: Add paneer cubes and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add richness: Add butter and cream. Stir well.
  5. Finish: Garnish with garam masala, kasoori methi, and coriander leaves.
